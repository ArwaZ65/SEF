Popular Destinations in Egypt

Cairo, the capital city of Egypt, offers incredible historical sites such as the Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and the Egyptian Museum.

Alexandria, a city on the Mediterranean coast, is known for its rich history, beautiful libraries, and the ancient Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa.

Luxor is often called the world’s greatest open-air museum, with the stunning Karnak Temple, Valley of the Kings, and many other ancient monuments.

Aswan is famous for its stunning Nile River scenery, temples such as Philae, and the impressive Aswan High Dam.
